Katrina Ashman

Hello! My name is Katrina Ashman and I am a UX design and artificial intelligence and robotics enthusiast. I got my start in software while attending a Montessori elementary school during which I learned to code short animation demonstration for my class using Java. I carried this on throughout high school during which time I worked on a number of passion projects involving creating websites to enable my classmates and I to study while solving puzzles. Additionally, while in high school, I began working on a project involving eye tracking and machine learning algorithms. This helped to grow my enthusiasm for artificial intelligence and robotics. I enjoy all things problem solving related and anything to spark the creative mind.
